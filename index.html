What is Orange? What is Gold? What is Blue? What is Green?

The more you know! No matter the color, you are beautiful.

Orange

The Adventurer

Those with Orange color personality strengths tend to be witty, spontaneous, generous, optimistic, eager and bold. They need fun, variety, stimulation and excitement. Freedom to act is also important to an Orange. Oranges have energy and like to bounce around to different projects or tasks. Charming and are the life of the party, Oranges are the uninhibited performers!

Gold

The Builder

Those with Gold color personality strengths tend to be loyal, dependable, organized, thorough, sensible, punctual and caring. They notice and remember facts, like directions and instructions, set deadlines and want them to be met. They enjoy check lists and checking things off the list! If your color is Gold, order, rules, respect, and dependability are important to you. Time is a key part of your life as well.

Want to explore the other colors? Go back!

Blue

The Relator

Those with Blue color personality strengths tend to be enthusiastic, sympathetic, communicative, compassionate, idealistic, sincere and imaginative. Someone with a blue personality tends to be calm and caring. If you have this personality type, you love to help and encourage others. As opposed to those with the Orange personality color, you are not impulsive or spontaneous - you always think before you speak and act and do everything at your own pace in your own time. You take time to process and share your feelings. Genuine and sincere, Blues are floating in a sea of tranquility!

Want to explore the other colors? Go back!

Green

The Planner

Those with Green color personality strengths tend to be perfectionistic, analytical, conceptual, cool, calm, inventive and logical. They seek knowledge and understanding as well as always looking for explanations and answers. They are good listeners who would make sure to offer help whenever needed. Their ability to see the big picture helps them make correct decisions; however, they dislike details and prefer to leave details to someone else, which allows them to be efficient business owners! If your color is Green, you are known to be expressive and emotional, yet, not afraid to show it out!
